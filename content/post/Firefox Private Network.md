Firefox Private Network is a desktop extension that helps secure and protect your connection everywhere you use Firefox.  Protect yourself from the hidden costs of free Wi-Fi, This extension is currently available to US desktop users only.  <!--more-->

**How it works**

Get started by adding the Private Network extension to Firefox for desktop and sign in with your Firefox account.
