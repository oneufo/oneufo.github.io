飞鸟集 Stray Birds

当你为错过太阳而哭泣的时候，你也要再错过群星了。

　　When you shed tears when you miss the sun, you also miss the stars.

纵然伤心，也不要愁眉不展，因为你不知是谁会爱上你的笑容。

　　Never frown, even when you are sad, because you never know who is falling in love with your smile.

有一个夜晚我烧毁了所有的记忆,从此我的梦就透明了,有一个早晨我扔掉了所有的昨天,从此我的脚步就轻盈了。

　　One night I burned all the memories,from now on my dream is transparent,one moming I threw away all yesterday,from now on my footsteps light.
